Banana Muffin Recipe

Hover to flip for ingredients 🍌

  • 2 bananas (mashed)
  • 1 cup flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F.
  2. Mash bananas and mix wet ingredients.
  3. Add dry ingredients and stir gently.
  4. Bake 20 minutes until golden brown.
